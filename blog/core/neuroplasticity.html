Neuroplasticity

Understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people greatly by giving them the knowledge that their brain can be strengthened and wasn't prewired with limitations. Increasing neuroplasticity is done through experiences or memories - the more something is done, the stronger the neural pathways are. I could incorporate the principles of neuroplasticity in my learning by finding code challenges that are repeating the same aspect I'm practicing, eg. functions, loops, methods etc. I found this website useful when researching neuroplasticity.

Growth Mindset

Growth mindset is the idea that people look at their struggles and challenges as a way to improve and get better. Instead of focusing on what's going wrong, to focus on the things that are going well. This is relevant because we're all learning new things, and if we weren't praising the things we're doing well, we would soon run out of motivation. I've been exposed to the idea of a growth mindset before, and although I don't have a fixed mindset that "I can't get better", I think I need to improve on recognising what I do well more often. I found this website useful when researching growth mindset.
